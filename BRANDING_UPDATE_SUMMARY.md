# KSB Primary Palette Branding Update

## Official Brand Colors Applied

### Primary Color Palette (from kogod.american.edu/brand-guidelines)

**Dynamic interpretation of AU's iconic blue and red:**

1. **AU Suffragist Purple** - #3C208C
   - Deep purple, official primary color
   
2. **KSB Inspire** - #7A2287
   - Purple magenta for innovation
   
3. **KSB Sustain** - #902178
   - Magenta representing sustainability
   - Used for: Dimension badges
   
4. **KSB Innovate** - #B02477
   - Pink magenta for innovation theme
   - Used for: Accent color, header border
   
5. **KSB Collaborate** - #C8246A
   - Bright pink for collaboration
   - Used for: AI outcome cards, borders, labels
   
6. **AU Red** - #ED193A
   - Vibrant institutional red
   - Used for: Assessment strategies, error states
   
7. **AU Intern Navy** - #182449
   - Dark navy for contrast
   - Used for: Button hover states
   
8. **AU Blue** - #004FA2
   - Official AU blue
   - Used for: Primary buttons, links, step numbers

## Application of Colors

### UI Elements
- **Primary Buttons**: AU Blue (#004FA2) → Navy (#182449) on hover
- **Header Accent Bar**: KSB Innovate pink magenta (#B02477)
- **Step Numbers**: AU Blue with purple shadow
- **Dimension Badges**: KSB Sustain magenta (#902178)
- **AI Outcome Cards**: KSB Collaborate pink (#C8246A) borders/labels
- **Assessment Sections**: AU Red (#ED193A) accents

### Typography
- **All text**: GT Walsheim (official primary font)
- **Removed**: Secondary fonts (Adobe Garamond, Road Rage)
- **Clean, modern**: Sans-serif throughout

## Brand Compliance

✅ **100% Official KSB Primary Palette**
✅ **Typography**: Official GT Walsheim font only
✅ **WCAG Contrast**: All combinations tested and accessible
✅ **Source**: https://kogod.american.edu/brand-guidelines

## Visual Impact

The new branding creates a vibrant, energetic look that:
- Reflects KSB's dynamic and innovative identity
- Uses the full spectrum of official brand colors
- Maintains professional contrast and readability
- Aligns with AU's iconic blue and red heritage
